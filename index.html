About Me

I create brand identities, user interfaces, packaging, websites, motion graphics, and everything in-between to create great brands.


I grew up being really creative and individualized. I would draw and make everything around me unique. But digital art and design really captured my attention.


When I was in high school, I decided to teach myself graphic design and started making t-shirts, posters, and logos for my fellow students. After graduating from high school, I worked as an in-house creative for five years, managing the brand identity, producing content, and directing photo and video shoots while also working as a freelancer.


I'm constantly trying to learn new things, so I taught myself programming to round out my knowledge. I still work as a freelancer for a wide range of clients, and most importantly, I continue to acquire new problem-solving techniques every day.


When I'm not using my laptop, I enjoy spending time outdoors by running, playing chess, skateboarding, playing table tennis and shooting hoops.
